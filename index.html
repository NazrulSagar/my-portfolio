About Me

Hello, This is Md. Nazrul Islam, and I am really passionate about teaching and technology. With over 17 years of experience as an IELTS and other ESL courses instructor, I've had the privilege of helping countless individuals achieve their English proficiency goals. My journey in education has been enriched by my roles as an entrepreneur, author, and lecturer.

As an entrepreneur, I've ventured into various aspects of education like United School, Gomoti Coaching, Kiddy Friday School, United English World & Comilla Mohanagar College, including writing and publishing books on English as a Second Language (ESL). These books have been valuable resources for students seeking to improve their language skills.

My commitment to education led me to serve as a senior lecturer in English at a reputable college for more than 6 years. During this time, I had the opportunity to guide students through their academic and linguistic journeys, fostering a love for the English language and effective communication.
